RESIDENZA CARDUCCI

an historical accomodation in the city of love

When you enter the walls of Verona you understand why Shakespeare fell in love with this charming and magical place.   The Adige river protects the heart of the city with a soft embrace and green hills crowned by ancient fortifications depart from the sides.  At night, the misty streets lit by lamp posts feel like a stage for a dramatic and passionate play.   At the Bed and Breakfast Residenza Carducci you can breathe the history of the city and experience the hospitality of a family that has been welcoming guests for generations.
